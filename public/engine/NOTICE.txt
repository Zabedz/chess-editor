Stockfish engine files

The stockfish-18-lite-single.js and stockfish-18-lite-single.wasm files served
from this directory are copied at install time (see scripts/copy-engine.mjs)
from the "stockfish" npm package, Stockfish.js by Nathan Rugg
(https://github.com/nmrugg/stockfish.js), which packages the Stockfish chess
engine compiled to WebAssembly.
